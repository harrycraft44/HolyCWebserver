Tribute to Terry A. Davis

Terry A. Davis (1969-2018) was known for creating TempleOS, an operating system written entirely by himself.

Terry also created HolyC, the programming lang created this site

“I wrote TempleOS, the 64-bit operating system, single-handedly. God said, 'Make it.'”
“TempleOS is God's temple. It runs HolyC, the language God told me to make.”
“Programming is fun. You get to create worlds.”

Despite everything he was a smart man that went far in life sad to see him gone and may he live on in this site.

Rest in peace, Terry.
